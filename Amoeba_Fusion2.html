🧬 Theory of Genetic Mutation

In this simulation, mutation occurs during Binary Fission. While asexual reproduction typically creates identical clones, environmental stressors or errors in DNA replication can lead to phenotypic changes. Here, a 20% mutation rate simulates Point Mutations that alter the proteins responsible for the cell's pigment, creating new color strains.

🌟 Proud Amoeba Facts

  • Immortality: Because they divide to reproduce, an amoeba doesn't "die" of old age—it literally becomes its children!
  • Giant Genomes: Some amoebae have 200 times more DNA than humans! They are genetically far more complex than us.
  • Brain Power: Despite having no brain, they can navigate mazes and remember where food was located.
